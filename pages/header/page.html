To add a header to a document double click the top margin of the page.
Now that we are in the header, anything we type will appear at the beginning of every page.
Many times we will want to display the page number. To do this select the page number menu in the design tab. In this demonstration we will choose top of page then plain number 3 to have a right aligned page number.
Now we can enter any other text we wish to appear in the header. In this case we will enter a name.
Similarly we can add a footer to our document by double clicking in the bottom margin.
To exit a header or footer simply click back into the body of the page.
